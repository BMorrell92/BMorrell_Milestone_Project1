About Jams Galatica FM

Greetings, I'm Kris and music is my passion. JAMS Galatica FM is a place where I bring you a carefully curated selection of high quality beats sourced from the forgotten nooks and crannies of the interweb.

What to expect?

I scan and scour the dusty corners of the web to collate, revive and repurpose long forgotten beats. If you're a fan of of early 90's Trance, Acid House and Jungle, then check out my mixes! You can also browse some media from past events and check out any upcoming events I will be a part of.
